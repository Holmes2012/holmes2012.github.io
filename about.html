About Catherine Cook School

Excellence in Education Since 1889

Our Story

Catherine Cook School has been providing exceptional education for over 130 years. Founded in 1889, we are committed to fostering academic excellence, creativity, and character development in our students.

Our mission is to nurture young minds in a supportive environment that encourages critical thinking, collaboration, and lifelong learning. We believe every student has unique potential that deserves to be cultivated and celebrated.
